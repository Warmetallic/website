MSc Project. The summer project during the studies in Aberdeen.

The Greig-Duncan folk song collection - is a collection of tradition and local music from the northeast of Scotland collated by Gavin Greig and Rev. James Bruce Duncan. As part of the MSc in Information Technology at the University of Aberdeen I had the project of creating a web application prototype to house, view and retrieve songs from the collection. This digitised version of the collection would allow scholars and enthusiasts to view the resource more easily. Dr Thomas McKean of the University of Aberdeen was the product owner. He wanted to create this application to improve access to the collection. Dr McKean had a vision of an intuitive and clear application prototype that catered to two key things - viewing and searching the Song Collection.
